Privacy Policy & Advertising Disclaimer

Last Updated: April 16, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to ModernBlog ("Website"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a transparent browsing experience. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information through our website and our use of advertising platforms.

2. Google AdSense & Third-Party Advertising

ModernBlog uses Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Please be aware of the following:

  • Third-Party Ad Serving: Google and other third-party vendors display ads based on prior website visits.
  • Cookie Usage: Google uses cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your previous visits to our website and other sites on the internet.
  • DART Cookie: Google's use of the DART cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and other sites on the Internet.
  • Personalized Ads: You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

3. Information We Collect

Automatic Information Collection

  • IP addresses and browsing behavior
  • Device information (browser type, operating system)
  • Page visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring/exit pages and links clicked
  • Search queries

Information from Ads and Tracking

  • Google AdSense collects data about your interactions with ads
  • Analytics data from Google Analytics
  • Cookies placed by advertising networks
  • Your interaction patterns with content

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To display relevant advertisements based on your interests
  • To improve website functionality and user experience
  • To analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To prevent fraudulent activity
  • To personalize content and recommendations

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience. These include:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for site functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how you use the site
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by Google AdSense and partner networks for ad targeting
  • Social Media Cookies: Enable social media sharing features

ModernBlog contains links to third-party websites and services. This Privacy Policy does not apply to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites. Please review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

We use Google Analytics to track website traffic and user behavior. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect data about how visitors use our site. This data is used to create reports about website usage. Google may also use this information for its own purposes in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

8. Your Privacy Rights

California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected about you
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out of the sale or sharing of personal information
  • Receive equal service despite exercising your privacy rights

General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union, you have rights regarding data processing including the right to access, rectify, erase, and port your data.

9. Cookies Management

You can manage cookies through your browser settings:

  • Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent
  • You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking
  • You can request removal of cookies at any time
  • Disabling cookies may affect website functionality

10. Opt-Out of Personalized Advertising

You can opt out of personalized advertising in several ways:

  • Visit Google Ad Settings: https://myaccount.google.com/ads
  • Visit the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page: https://optout.networkadvertising.org
  • Visit the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out page: https://optout.aboutads.info
